The AI Toolbox is a structured guide built for product design students navigating projects from concept to launch. Each tool is matched to a specific phase of the design process so you always know exactly what to use — and when.

Stop scrolling through listicles of "best AI tools." Start building with the right ones, at the right time, with guidance tied to your actual milestones.
