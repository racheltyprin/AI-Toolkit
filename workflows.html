Step 3: Data (10 min)

In Glide or Softr, create a spreadsheet or Airtable base and connect it to your app.

Workflow #2: Research to Demo Creator

Step 1: Research (10 min)

In Perplexity, ask about competitors and user complaints in your space. Note gaps.

Step 2: Sketch (10 min)

Uizard or Figma: describe your concept for AI-generated screens, or use a Figma template.

Step 3: Build (10 min)

In Framer, describe your concept and generate the site, then customize copy and features.

Step 4: Polish (5 min)

Add real content, get a shareable link, test on mobile and desktop.
